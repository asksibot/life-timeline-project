    How to Use the Pictogram Search Assistant

    Welcome to the Pictogram Assistant! It is designed to help find and utilize pictographic symbols from ARASAAC's extensive library.

    Steps to Use the Assistant:

    1. Select a Topic: Choose one topic that best describes your needs from the available options. Your choice generates keywords to try.
    2. Select Keywords: Choose relevant keywords related to your selected topic or enter your own specific keywords separated by commas.
    3. Generate Search Terms: Click the "Generate Search Terms" button to compile your selections into unique search terms.
    4. View Pictograms: The assistant will display relevant pictograms based on your keyword.
    5. Create a Slideshow: Select the pictograms you wish to include in your slideshow by checking the box next to each pictogram. Once selected, click the "Create Slideshow" button to view your selected pictograms in a slideshow format.

    What Can Pictograms Be Used For?

    Pictograms help to enhance communication, especially for individuals with speech or language difficulties. They can be utilized in various settings, including:

    • Developing communication boards or systems.
    • Creating visual schedules and routines.
    • Enhancing instructional materials and learning aids.
    • Facilitating social interactions and emotional expression.
    • Supporting daily living activities and independence.

    How to Use the Slideshow

    Follow these steps to effectively utilize your slideshow:

    1. Select Pictograms: Browse through the displayed pictograms and check the boxes next to the ones you want to include in your slideshow.
    2. Create Slideshow: Click the "Create Slideshow" button to compile your selected pictograms into a slideshow format.
    3. Preview Slideshow: Use the "Prev" and "Next" buttons to navigate through your slideshow and ensure it meets your expectations.
    4. Save or Download: You can use the slideshow directly from the site or download it by following the provided instructions within the slideshow modal.
    5. Use the Slideshow: Utilize the slideshow in your desired setting, whether it's for communication boards, visual schedules, instructional materials, or any other purpose.
