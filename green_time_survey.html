Green Time Survey

Understanding Green Time

Green Time refers to the time individuals spend in natural environments, such as parks, forests, gardens, or any green spaces. Engaging with nature has been shown to significantly improve mood, reduce stress, enhance cognitive function, and promote overall well-being.

By participating in this survey, you'll gain insights into how much time you spend in natural settings and understand the potential impact of nature on your mental and emotional health.
